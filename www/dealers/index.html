Why Voosia?

Here at Voosia we work with you to make sure we give your customers the money they need in the best possible way and most importantly when they need it! That means they can apply for a loan and get their money while still sitting in your showroom.

The team at Voosia have years of proven experience in both the motor industry and that of financial services. This has meant we have become the ‘trusted’ party for both consumers and dealers in finding the right lenders. We act as a financial services broker and have spent years building one of the most advanced platforms on the market for matching customers to lenders. 

This all happens in real time at the click of button.
